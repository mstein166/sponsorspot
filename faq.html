What is SponsorSpot?

SponsorSpot is a platform that connects local businesses with consumers through special deals and promotions.

How do I get started?

To get started, simply sign up for an account and browse the available deals. You can also create and manage your own deals if you're a business.

Is there a fee to use SponsorSpot?

There is no fee for consumers to use SponsorSpot. Businesses may incur fees depending on the features they choose to use.
